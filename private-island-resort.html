Melly Island Resort

Melly Island resort is a private island located in Maldives.We are surrounded by over a thousand small coral islands not forgetting the Great Indian Ocean.When you have a stay at Melly Island resort you have a guarantee of seeing the natives of the island and have a taste of different cultures.

Here are examples of things you will experience at Melly Island resort

  • Deep sea diving
  • Scuba diving
  • Snorkeling
  • Traditional cultural dances and food
  • Go-karting
  • Bunjee jumping
  • And many more!
